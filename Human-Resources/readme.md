## 📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on Human Resources Analytics using Power BI to transform employee data into actionable business insights.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of workforce demographics, compensation, tenure, and organizational structure, enabling HR teams and business leaders to better understand employee distribution, workforce trends, and key people-related metrics.

The dataset used is synthetic but designed to simulate a realistic HR environment, allowing the development of meaningful HR analytics solutions and executive reporting.

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

Organizations often struggle to obtain a consolidated view of their workforce across multiple departments and business units.

The objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor workforce demographics
- Analyze compensation trends
- Understand employee tenure patterns
- Identify workforce distribution across departments
- Support strategic HR decision-making

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of the workforce.

### Key KPIs

- Total Employees
- Average Salary
- Average Bonus
- Average Tenure
- Number of Business Units
- Department Distribution

### Business Questions Answered

- How is the workforce distributed across the organization?
- Which departments have the highest employee concentration?
- What is the average employee tenure?
- Which business units receive the highest compensation?

---

## 2️⃣ Demographics Analysis

Focuses on workforce diversity and employee composition.

### Key Metrics

- Gender Ratio
- Average Employee Age
- Age Distribution
- Ethnicity Distribution
- Workforce Composition by Department

### Business Questions Answered

- What does the gender balance look like?
- Which age groups dominate the workforce?
- How diverse is the organization?
- How do demographics vary across departments?

---

## 3️⃣ Compensation Analysis

Provides visibility into employee remuneration patterns.

### Key Metrics

- Average Salary by Business Unit
- Bonus-to-Salary Ratio
- Compensation Distribution
- Salary Comparisons by Demographics

### Business Questions Answered

- Which business units have the highest average salaries?
- How are bonuses distributed across the workforce?
- Are there noticeable compensation differences across demographic groups?

---

## 4️⃣ Workforce Tenure Analysis

Analyzes employee retention and organizational experience.

### Key Metrics

- Average Tenure by Department
- Top 10 Longest-Serving Employees
- Employee Experience Distribution

### Business Questions Answered

- Which departments retain employees the longest?
- Where might retention challenges exist?
- How experienced is the current workforce?
